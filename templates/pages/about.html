Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Results

HOMEXO was founded with a singular vision: to make premium property buying and selling as seamless as it is exciting. What began as a boutique advisory firm has grown into one of India's most respected real estate platforms — trusted by families, investors, and NRIs alike.

We understand that buying a home is one of life's most significant decisions. That's why every client relationship at HOMEXO is built on genuine understanding, meticulous research, and honest advice — not just sales targets. Our advisors are trained differently: they listen first, then guide.

Today, HOMEXO operates across 12 cities, representing premium projects from India's leading developers and maintaining an exclusive resale portfolio of vetted luxury properties. From first-time buyers to seasoned investors managing multi-crore portfolios, we serve every client with the same precision and care.
