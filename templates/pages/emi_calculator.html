Understanding Your Loan

What every home buyer should know

01

What is EMI?

An Equated Monthly Instalment is the fixed monthly amount paid to your lender, covering both principal and interest.

02

Front-Loaded Interest

In early years, a larger part of EMI goes to interest. Over time, the principal component gradually increases.

03

Prepayment Benefit

Early part-prepayments can significantly reduce total interest and shorten tenure, often saving substantial cost.

The Formula

How EMI is calculated

EMI = [ P x R x (1+R)N ] / [ (1+R)N - 1 ]
EEquated monthly instalment
PPrincipal loan amount
RMonthly interest rate (Annual / 12)
NTenure in months
