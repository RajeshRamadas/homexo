Legacy
About & Legacy
1986
Founded in Bengaluru
Razack Sattar establishes Prestige Group with a vision to bring quality real estate development to South India. First project: a small residential layout in Bengaluru's then-emerging suburbs.
1995
First Landmark Township
Prestige Acropolis, a 350-unit residential complex, sets a new benchmark for gated community living in Bengaluru and establishes the group's commitment to quality construction.
2010
Listed on BSE & NSE
Prestige Group goes public, raising ₹1,200 Cr in its IPO. The listing brings institutional transparency and enables large-scale project development across India.
2015
Prestige Shantiniketan Completes
One of India's largest integrated townships at 105 acres — with 7,000 homes, a mall, hotels, and a convention centre — is fully delivered on schedule.
2021
Blackstone Partnership & Commercial Sale
Prestige sells its commercial, retail, and hospitality assets to Blackstone for ₹9,160 Cr — one of India's largest real estate transactions. Proceeds fuel an aggressive residential growth phase.
2024
₹15,000 Cr Sales Milestone
Prestige records its highest-ever annual pre-sales of ₹15,000 Cr, driven by strong demand for its luxury and premium residential projects in Bengaluru, Mumbai, Hyderabad, and Chennai.
